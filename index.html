Dev EnChroma [DEC]

Turn Nature into Your Color Palette

As a seasoned software developer, I’ve spent years working with color codes—hex values that shape the aesthetics, accessibility, and psychological impact of digital experiences. The right color palette doesn’t just make a website look good; it enhances user engagement and ensures inclusivity.

That’s why I created Dev EnChroma—a pocket-sized device designed for developers to capture colors from the real world and instantly convert them into hex values. Whether you're designing a web app, mobile interface, or digital artwork, Dev EnChroma helps you find inspiration beyond the screen. Just press a button, scan any surface, and get the exact color code to use in your projects.

Currently, I’m developing the prototype, and I need your support to bring this vision to life. If you believe in the power of blending nature with technology, consider making a small donation below to keep the progress going.
